## About Me

<h1 align="center">¡Chulla vida! (Quiteño saying: "Carpe diem" / "Life is one")</h1>
<h3 align="center"><em>An architect exploring the world from the programmers' perspective</em></h3>

A native of Quito who ended up in Saint Petersburg, mixing architecture, urban planning, and programming.  
I like to understand how forms, cities, and things work, to teach what I learn, to learn from those who know, to exchange information, and to experiment with algorithms to give a foundation to form and function.  
Passionate about Russian cultural heritage and geopolitical analysis, always seeking to connect Latin American thought with Eastern traditions.  
I am not married to any tool (maybe only Rhino and Grasshopper). Here you'll find texts, PDFs, code, Grasshopper definitions, or whatever comes up.
